THEORETICAL SUMMARY
---------------------------------------

Please, think about everything you've learnt about this opening. Now it's time to systematize this new information, and  summarize the main ideas. Try to answer the following questions.

=======================
1) In general, what are the best squares for your pieces in this opening?
2) What are the typical manoeuvres of your pieces?
3) What are the main plans for White and Black?
4) Are there any standard tactical ideas for White and Black?
5) Are there any typical endgame positions for this opening? Are they good for you?
=======================

After that please go to the e-book, find the corresponding chapter in the section  "THEORETICAL SUMMARY FOR THE OPENINGS", and study it.
